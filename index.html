CityDAO 101

We are a community-driven organization that aims to create a platform that informs and engages individuals in the CityDAO ecosystem. Our goal is to provide accurate and easily accessible information that will help citizens make informed decisions and create an inclusive and informative platform that attracts and educates newcomers.

CityDAO

CityDAO is an organization focused on decentralized asset ownership on the blockchain, starting with a 40-acre land in Wyoming. The land is represented as an NFT, and Citizen NFT holders gain citizenship in CityDAO, allowing community access and governance. The ultimate objective is to create a decentralized city governed by citizens through a DAO, working towards a more democratic society despite challenges. CityDAO aims to leverage blockchain technology for transparent decision-making and collective ownership. By providing citizens with a say in governance and asset management, the project strives to redefine traditional notions of city governance and empower its members.
